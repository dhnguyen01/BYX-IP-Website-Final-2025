About Island Party

Island Party is a family-friendly music concert including some of the nation’s most popular bands, held locally at Wolf Pen Creek Amphitheater.

In addition, there are food trucks, vendors, a speaker that shares the gospel, and plenty of family-friendly activities for the whole community to enjoy. Every year, Island Party attracts thousands of attendees from across the state and makes a lasting impact through local and international philanthropy. It serves to connect the community as a whole, hosting college students and permanent residents alike.

This year, Island Party will be held on March 29th, 2025, and we are currently in the early processes of garnering sponsors.

Our Mission Partner

Living Water International exists to demonstrate the love of God by helping communities acquire desperately needed clean water, and to experience “living water” — the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Which alone satisfies the deepest thirst. We love partnering with Living Water International, knowing that every dollar raised helps communities gain access to clean water and hope.
